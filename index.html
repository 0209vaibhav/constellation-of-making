what

A 3D constellation of dots, each representing a fragment of a project — its title, concept, methods, and ideas. Every dot is a thought. Every cluster is a project.

why

To map a body of work not as a list, but as a living field — where relationships between ideas matter as much as the projects themselves.

read it
  • Hover over any dot to reveal its project and connected fragments
  • Use the filters to isolate layers — titles, concepts, methods, and more
  • Rotate, zoom, and pan to explore the field from any angle
  • Click a highlighted dot to visit the project (where available)
made with
  • Three.js — 3D rendering and instanced dot mesh
  • OrbitControls — rotate, zoom, and pan
  • Vanilla JS — hover, raycasting, filter logic
  • HTML & CSS — UI layer, tooltips, filters
  • IBM Plex Mono + Inter — typography
